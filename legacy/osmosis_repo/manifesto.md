# The OS/mosis Manifesto
*A Statement of Core Principles, Integrity, and Non-Negotiable Design*

## I. The Prime Directive
1. Correctness First: Maintain a verifiable and true state.
2. Clarity Always: Distill hardware complexity into minimalist software interfaces.

## II. The Panic Covenant
Upon detecting an unrecoverable fault, the system will immediately and decisively halt, communicating its final, clear state. Silence is preferable to false execution.
